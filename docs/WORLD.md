# WORLD — Geography of the Shattered Coast

*The physical world — regions, cities, dungeons, and points of interest.*

---

## Overview

The Shattered Coast is a wedge of land roughly 200 km × 200 km, bounded by the Serpent Sea to the west and the Iron Mountains to the east. It was the westernmost province of the Aetherian Empire, and therefore the region that preserves the most ruins, the deepest dungeons, and the highest concentration of Fracture scars.

## Regions

### 1. The Verdant March

*Heartland. Farmland, rolling hills, scattered villages.*

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Terrain** | Gentle hills, open grassland, scattered copses |
| **Weather** | Temperate. Rain 3-4 days/week. Foggy mornings. |
| **Day/Night** | Standard 12h/12h |
| **Settlements** | Havenmoor (capital), 6 villages, 12 hamlets |
| **Dungeons** | 3 crypts, 2 ruins, 1 temple (flooded) |
| **Threat level** | Low — goblins, bandits, wild dogs |
| **Resources** | Timber, grain, livestock, clay |

**Notable locations:**
- **Havenmoor** — The largest settlement on the coast. Walled city of 8,000. Seat of the Council. Headquarters of the Iron Covenant.
- **Ashford Village** — Farming community. Known for its mead. Recent crypt bloom.
- **The Broken Bridge** — Ruined empire bridge spanning the River Amber. Troll lair beneath.

### 2. The Iron Mountains

*Eastern highlands. Ore-rich peaks, goblin territory.*

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Terrain** | Steep slopes, rocky outcrops, alpine meadows |
| **Weather** | Cold. Snow above 1,500m. Sudden storms. |
| **Day/Night** | 10h/14h in winter |
| **Settlements** | Brightforge (underground), 3 mining camps |
| **Dungeons** | 5 mines, 2 caves, 1 ancient forge |
| **Threat level** | High — goblins, cave trolls, harpies, earth elementals |
| **Resources** | Iron, silver, coal, gems, crystal |

**Notable locations:**
- **Brightforge** — The forge-city, carved into Mount Velus. Steam-powered machinery. Gnomish engineering at its peak.
- **The Crystal Caverns** — Deep cave system. Bioluminescent crystals. Ember Circle sanctuary.
- **Grimfang Peak** — Highest peak. Harpy queen's nest. Rumored sky-temple at summit.
- **The Deep Tunnels** — Goblin-excavated warrens connecting the mountain range. Ambush hazard.

### 3. The Whisperwood

*Southern forest. Ancient, magical, dangerous.*

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Terrain** | Dense canopy, winding streams, hidden clearings |
| **Weather** | Humid. Frequent rain. Mist most mornings. |
| **Day/Night** | 8h/16h under canopy |
| **Settlements** | 2 ranger outposts, 3 druid groves |
| **Dungeons** | 4 ruins (overgrown), 2 caves, 1 sunken temple |
| **Threat level** | Medium-high — wolves, bears, spiders, treants, spriggans |
| **Resources** | Rare herbs, exotic timber, game |

**Notable locations:**
- **The Sunken Temple** — Pre-Fracture temple to a forgotten water god. Now half-flooded. Ember Circle watches it.
- **Heartwood Grove** — The largest cluster of ancient oaks. Druid council meets here under the full moon.
- **The Witch's Fen** — Swamp in the forest's heart. Will-o'-wisps lead travelers to their doom. A witch (real? legend?) lives in a hut on stilts.
- **Ranger Post Thornwall** — The Iron Covenant's token presence. Three rangers who mostly stay out of the forest's business.

### 4. The Grim Wastes

*Northern tundra. Fracture-scarred, haunted.*

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Terrain** | Barren tundra, frozen lakes, jagged rock formations |
| **Weather** | Extreme cold. Blizzards common. |
| **Day/Night** | 6h/18h in winter |
| **Settlements** | None permanent. 2 seasonal hunter camps. |
| **Dungeons** | 3 ice caves, 2 frozen ruins, 1 Fracture scar |
| **Threat level** | Extreme — frost wraiths, yetis, ice golems, undead legions |
| **Resources** | Ice crystal (magic reagent), mammoth ivory, ancient relics |

**Notable locations:**
- **The Fracture Scar** — A visible wound in reality. The sky twists above it. Creatures that emerge from it are not entirely natural. The Veiled Hand maintains a watch post.
- **Frosthold** — Ruined empire fortress, now inhabited by ice golems. Its basement connects to a deeper dungeon level.
- **The Frozen Archive** — A Sunken Archive outpost. A single librarian maintains a collection of pre-Fracture texts in a magically warmed bunker.
- **Mammoth Graveyard** — A valley of bones. Yeti territory. Valuable ivory if you can survive the harvest.

### 5. The Serpent Coast

*Western shoreline. Cliffs, beaches, sea caves.*

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Terrain** | Cliffs (north), sandy beaches (south), sea caves |
| **Weather** | Windy, salty. Storms frequent in autumn. |
| **Day/Night** | Standard |
| **Settlements** | Duskhollow (port city), 4 fishing villages |
| **Dungeons** | 3 sea caves, 2 wreck sites, 1 submerged temple |
| **Threat level** | Medium — sirens, sea serpents, pirates, cliff striders |
| **Resources** | Fish, pearls, salvage, smuggled goods |

**Notable locations:**
- **Duskhollow** — The shadow port. No laws, no guards, no questions. Every faction has a representative here. The Nightfather rules from an unmarked tower.
- **The Serpent's Jaw** — A narrow strait where sea serpents nest. Ships pay tribute or risk passage. Local legend: a drowned empire city below.
- **Tidefall Village** — Fishing community. Once a year, the tide recedes further than usual, revealing a submerged dungeon entrance.
- **The Lighthouse** — Automated (magic crystal). The lightkeeper disappeared a decade ago. The light still burns. No one goes inside.

### 6. The Amber Valley

*Central river valley. Rich, contested.*

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Terrain** | River valley, terraced vineyards, orchards |
| **Weather** | Mild. Best climate on the coast. |
| **Day/Night** | Standard |
| **Settlements** | 3 large villages, numerous farms |
| **Dungeons** | 2 crypts, 1 empire ruin, 1 hidden shrine |
| **Threat level** | Low-medium — bandits, river monsters |
| **Resources** | Wine, fruit, grain, fish |

**Notable locations:**
- **River Amber** — The lifeline of the coast. Traded along its length from Brightforge to Havenmoor to Duskhollow.
- **The Amber Falls** — A spectacular 40m waterfall. The Ember Circle says magic concentrates here. A cave behind the falls contains a shrine.
- **Vineheart** — Wealthiest village. Known for its amber wine, aged in dungeon crypts for flavor. The crypts are safe (mostly).
